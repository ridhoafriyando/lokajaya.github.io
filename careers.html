Crayon’s mission is to provide enterprise and mid-sized businesses with a complete, real-time picture of what their competitors are up to—delivering valuable insights to key departments in a range of accessible formats. So they can quickly see and seize opportunities, and build a sustainable business advantage.

When you join Crayon, you’ll become part of a vibrant and passionate team that values all perspectives and enjoys learning and improving every day. If you think you’re a good fit for what we’re building here, then we’d love to hear from you.

What we do

Competitive Intelligence (CI) is specific and verifiable information about companies you compete with in your business environment. It is the process of capturing, analyzing, and distributing data to various stakeholders—who use that information to inform strategic planning and inspire actions to grow their business.

Founded in 2015, Crayon is the established leader in the CI software space, using AI and machine learning to provide customers with the data and insights they need to make real-time decisions and create a sustainable competitive
advantage in their market.

Core Values

As the undisputed leader in the Competitive Intelligence space, our team uses technology and software to solve problems daily. We believe in the power of collaboration and love to work hard while having fun. Crayon is passionately opposed to bureaucracy, politics, and BS, and we have an open-door policy, always.

Here are the five key values that we believe are the recipe for success:

  • Solving for the customer and helping them be heroes
  • Thinking clearly yet maintaining a bias towards action
  • Working hard but striking a healthy balance
  • Empowering individuals with transparency and feedback
  • Building an inclusive team with committed individuals

Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion

Diversity, equity, and inclusion (DE&I) are at the core of who Crayon is, and our commitment to these values is unwavering. We strongly believe that the most successful teams are diverse teams. Our culture is eliminating barriers so that everyone can do their best work, and we strive for an environment where all employees feel a sense of belonging.

We furthered our DE&I commitment by partnering with MassTLC’s Tech Compact for Social Justice and Gaingels — the world’s largest investor network focused on diversity in venture capital — in our Series B funding round. We also established an employee-led DE&I group that meets twice per month. Crayon is an equal opportunity employer and prohibits discrimination and harassment of any kind.
