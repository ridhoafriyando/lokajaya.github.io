Klaviyo Case Study

Written by Lauren Kersanske | Mar 16, 2022 9:04:12 PM

The opportunity

Competitive intelligence at Klaviyo was done using a Google Sheet, which was a manual process for collecting data and distributing it to the team. Information was constantly out of date, not reflective of industry changes, and hard to distribute across the team.

The RESULTS

Crayon has become the centralized hub of competitive intelligence for Klaviyo, and competitive win rates have increased as much as 59% since rolling out Crayon Battlecards.
